Subtraction Word Problems

Subtraction word problems can be a little tricky because the order of the numbers in the story doesn’t always match their order in the number sentence.

What You Need to Know

Jude ate 6 donuts and Alex ate 11. How many more donuts did Alex eat?

The appropriate number sentence for this story is:

11 – 6 = 5

6 comes before the 11 in the word problem, but in the number sentence the 11 comes before the 6.

Alex ate 5 more donuts than Jude.
