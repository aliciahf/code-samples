Number Sentences

Converting word sentences into number sentences is a key step in successfully solving word problems.

What You Need to Know

PROBLEM

Martha brought 2 sodas, 1 orange juice, and 1 bottle of water to the game. How many drinks did she bring total?

STEP 1

The word sentence in the problem can be re-written as the following number sentence:

2 + 1 + 1 = _

ANSWER

Martha brought 4 drinks to the game.
