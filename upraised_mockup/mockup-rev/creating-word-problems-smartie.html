Creating Word Problems

Creating word problems involving real world situations is a fun next step after students understand how to solve them independently.

What You Need to Know

STEP 1

It’s helpful to provide a starting point number, like 7. Then, students can create word problems using the given number:

Samantha had 3 apples and Mark had 4. How many did they have together?

TIP

It’s important to remember which terms refer to addition and which refer to subtraction.

  • Addition: total, together, sum

  • Subtraction: more than/less than, fewer, difference
